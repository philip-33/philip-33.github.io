Image Optimization

This exercise was straightforward and something I've done many times in the past for client websites. The need to balance resolution, quality, and load time is something that I've known is important, but haven't mastered.

The original resolution of the image was 2250 x 1529 and 464 KB

After cropping and resizing, the image was 750 x 524 and 85KB

After cropping, resizing, and compressing to '50', the image was 750 x 524 and 43KB

Image before

Image after cropping/resizing

Image after compression

I used Pixlr since it is very close to Photoshop in terms of interface and layout and that's what I'm most familiar with. www.jpeg-optimizer.com/ is the site that I used to change the compression level.

Image manipulation is part of the skillset that was necessary for me to pick up while making websites. Although definitely not skilled at Photoshop, I've had a lot of success with image wrangling for my clients.
