Web Hosting

As others have mentioned, wix.com is one of the most popular free web hosting sites. My only experience of it was trying to help a friend make their wix site less bad a few years ago, and we both gave up. According to this article, it sounds like it's better now but not a whole lot. something something get what you pay for.

Paid solutions are one's that I've spent a lot of time with. After moving around through several hosts over the years (godaddy, nixihost, hostgator, squarespace, dreamhost) I finally found SiteGround.com which is the best for me. Their tech support responses are quick and courteous so it's always easy to get things done, and their reseller hosting is done by a token system for each site instead of a large yearly payment, which is a huge benefit for low-volume resellers like me.
