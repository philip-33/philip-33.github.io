Development Skills

The development skills presented here deal with topics that a developer needs to know about. They are not organized by any particular domain such as programming or design, but they are all considered necessary to understand. Some of the topics here, like UI vs. UX or template analysis are not perfectly straightforward, but understanding the nuance or having a degree of skill is the purpose of their inclusion. Other items like full stack vs. front end and CDNs are relatively simple and equally critical at the risk of having a fundamental flaw in the way you think about the whole of the field.

There are many other skills that could fall within this, and basic programming/development has been mostly omitted since this category is less about basic developer skills such as knowing a programming language and more focused on beginning to show how those skills are best put to use.
